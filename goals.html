Goal Setting

Learn how to set meaningful goals and track your progress daily.

Why Goals Matter

Setting goals gives you focus, motivation, and a roadmap for success. It helps you prioritize and measure progress in academics, health, and personal growth.

Long-term Goals

Big dreams for your future like academic achievements, career milestones, or personal development targets.

Short-term Goals

Daily or weekly goals that build up to your long-term objectives.

Daily Goal Tracker

Add your goals for today and track your progress visually.
