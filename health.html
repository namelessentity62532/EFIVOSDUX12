Health & Fitness

Guides for nutrition, exercise, and overall well-being for teens.

Daily Exercise Plan

  • 10 min warm-up
  • 15 min cardio
  • 15 min strength exercises (bodyweight)
  • 5 min cool down/stretching

Nutrition Tips

  • Eat 3 balanced meals + 2 healthy snacks
  • Include fruits, vegetables, and proteins
  • Stay hydrated: 6-8 glasses of water
  • Limit sugary drinks and junk food
